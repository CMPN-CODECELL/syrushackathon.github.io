Frequently Asked Questions

What is a hackathon?

A hackathon is a social coding event that brings computer programmers and other interested people together to improve upon or build a new program for a certain problem statement. Syrus is a type of Altruistic hackathon where participants work to find solutions for problems such as public transport systems, education and disaster response, security, etc.

Who all can participate in Syrus Hackathon?

The Syrus Hackathon is only available for the Computer Department of VESIT currently in Second Year or Third Year.

Do we get any certificate for participation?

All participants will recieve a participation certificate as well as goodies sponsored by GitHub. The winners will receive a Grand Prize consisting of a cash prize and various merchandise. However it is completely necessary for the team leader of each team to be present physically on the two days in the college.

Will you be providing us with any hardware?

Participants are required to bring their own laptops and charging devices. We will not be providing them with any hardware.

Where and when will be the competition be conducted?

The competition will be conducted on 18th and 19th of March 2020. It will be a 34 hour long competition. Participants are required to be present on the first day compulsorily in college.

Can I participate if I don't have a team?

Individual participants are eligible for the competition. However maximum three members are allowed to be in a team.
